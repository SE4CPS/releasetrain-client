Workshop Invitation

Software Engineering in Industry 4.0 Ecosystems

About the Workshop

You are cordially invited to participate in the workshop on Software Engineering in Industry 4.0 Ecosystems as part of the 7th International Conference on Emerging Data and Industry (EDI40).

In this workshop, we will explore the latest developments and challenges in software engineering within the context of Industry 4.0 ecosystems. The workshop provides a platform for researchers and industry experts to exchange ideas, present their work, and discuss innovations in this rapidly evolving field.

We welcome contributions in the form of papers.

Important Information

  • Date: April 2024
  • Location: Hasselt, Belgium
  • Conference: The 7th International Conference on Emerging Data and Industry (EDI40)

Submission Requirements

  • Paper should be no more than 6 pages in length.
  • Camera-ready due date will be updated soon.
  • Paper shall follow the conference paper formatting guidelines.
